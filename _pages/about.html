Who are we?

Malayalam Learning is an unofficial global community where we gather and present information about Malayalam Language Learning classes around the globe.

Yes, there are people who are eager to learn Malayalam language but struggling to find a proper channel for the same. We are trying to collect such information

Please note that as this is not an official site for any orgnization but a community driven initiative, it is also stated in the disclaimer.
